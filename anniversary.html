Hungry Jacks

I think this was the first thing we did when we met at SM Bicutan. We went to Burger King to have a meal. It's nostalgic to think about those times when I used to work hard just to save money. Hahaha. And when we met, we would simply eat at fast-food restaurants.

Furthermore, at this time we ate because we were hungry and the food was delicious. I don't know why, maybe because it was a rare occasion for us to eat at Burger King, but I really enjoyed their burgers because of the aroma. I don't know how they make their burgers, but they taste like smoked patties, and that's why I find them so tasty. And their fries are also delicious. I remember trying their onion rings, but I didn't like them because they only looked so appetizing in the pictures, and I was expecting them to taste like calamari. Hahaha. Since they're also rings, I regretted ordering them. LOL.

There are not actually many things to talk about with this story. However, it is stil one of the memorable things we did in order to continue our story. So, I apologize for not having much of this timeline.
