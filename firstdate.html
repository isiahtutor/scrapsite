On this remarkable day, after enduring the heartache of our break-up, we found ourselves on our very first date since our hearts mended from the paint of separation. It was a date that we would always remember, filled with precious moments and sweet memories. What made it even more unforgettable was the fact that Mama May had a suspicion that we were secretly seeing each other again. Little did she know that our love was slowly reigniting, like flame flickering back to life. Our hearts were drawn together once more, ready to embark on a new chapter of our love story.

Besides, it's rare for me to browse through shoes, even though I didn't find any thing appealing in the store. Moreover, considering we had just come out of a long and strict lockdown, those moments marked my return to going out to malls again.

We were basically just romaing around the store, and you were trying to capture a good picture of me so I could post something on my socials while I was trying to look for a good shoes. It's like you were my secret stalker on the side, because you were letting me browse through the shoes while taking hidden pictures of me to make it look candid. You're such a cutie. Hahahaha.

At this time, our activities were fairly limited to look at shoes and finding to capture a good photo of myself to share on my Instagram. Honestly, I am genuinely appreciative that you were the one who came into my life once again, because it was at that moment when I started frequently posting pictures of me on my Instagram account. Since previously, I had little to no one taking pictures of me, and when someone do, the results were often unflattering or some are unattractive. Hahaha.

note: I really apologize for this matter because I couldn't exaclty remember what happened on this store. Although, what I just mostly remember is that we were just trying to look for a good shoes to put it on our wishlist.
